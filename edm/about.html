MANILA / EDM · ABOUT

A community node for Manila.

Manila EDM is an independent, English-first directory for artists, groups, and local electronic music connections around Metro Manila.

Created and stewarded

Created in 2026 and stewarded by JOSH.sol and MidnightRider.sol.

The directory is part of an emerging Riderverse pattern: reusable tools and protocols that independent communities can adapt to their own local ethos without merging their data or identity.

How this stays independent

  • Manila records are maintained separately from other community directories.
  • Local context and steward review come before public display.
  • The public site is a lightweight rendered view, not the source-of-truth database.
